Built a dual-interface AI assistant (CLI + Streamlit) for clinics to streamline patient interaction and operational efficiency. Enabled the assistant to answer questions about clinic hours, insurance, appointment scheduling, and general medical topics using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines. Developed dynamic query routing between a local FAQ retrieval tool and a PubMed-based medical retrieval tool to deliver accurate, contextually relevant responses. Integrated real-time appointment booking and logging with Google Calendar and Sheets, ensuring availability checks and preventing scheduling conflicts.

Implemented semantic search with FAISS and Sentence-Transformers embeddings to enable fast, relevant retrieval from both clinic and medical corpora. Leveraged large language models (Mistral-7B) for natural language understanding and human-like response generation.
